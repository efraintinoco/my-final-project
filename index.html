Efrain Tinoco

Hello everyone! My name is Efrain Tinoco. Professionally, I have over 20 years of experience with Ford Motor Credit. I currently supervise a diverse team of 12 CSR’s, which I help train and motivate towards individual and group goals. I ensure daily tasks and functions are completed accurately, timely, and per Company Policy and Procedure. I am a self-starter, and detailed oriented, with great written and verbal communications skills.

Personally, I am married to a beautiful wife and I'm a father of two wonderful teenagers. I was born and raised in Dallas, TX, which explains why I am the biggest Dallas Cowboys fan you will ever meet! I am of Mexican-American descent, and I am very proud of my culture. As such, I make it a point to celebrate Day of the Dead every year, which is one of the most important holidays in the Latino community.

Aspiring Software Engineer !

I’m Interested in a career as a software engineer, because of the long-term outlook that being a software engineer presents within the company. As the company continues to evolve and places an emphasis on “Modernizing Everywhere”, I know software engineering will be in high demand, which can potentially open up a completely different career path for me, while remaining at Ford Credit. Having worked here for over 20 years, I have been exploring different options, within the company, that can help me expand my knowledge and contribute to the team in a different way. I think the Learn to Code program does just that and is a perfect fit for me!
